Garri Recipe

Garri is pan fried cassava flakes which is very popular in West Africa. Soaked Garri ‘cereal’ is definitely a must try for anyone visiting West Africa for the first time.

Ingredient:

  • garri
  • water
  • sugar
  • groundnut
  • milk
  • ice

Steps To Take:

  1. Pour the garri cereal into a bowl
  2. Pour desired amount of water into the cereal
  3. Add desired amount of Sugar
  4. Add desired amount of milk
  5. Add desired amount of groundnut
  6. Add 7 pieces of ice just to make the cereal chilly
  7. And garri is fit for consumption...

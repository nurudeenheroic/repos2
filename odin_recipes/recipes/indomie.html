Indomie Noodles Recipe

Noodle meal, often featuring Indomie, is a quick and flavorful dish that is very popular in Nigeria. It's typically made by boiling Indomie noodles with its seasoning, sometimes adding ingredients like fried eggs, vegetables (onions, green beans, carrots), and sometimes even seafood or meat. The noodles are often fried or stir-fried, and the overall dish is known for its spicy and savory taste.

Ingrdients:

  • pack of noodles
  • 1 cup of chopped desired vegetables
  • 1 tablespoon of cooking oil

Steps To Take:

  1. Remove seasoning from the indomie pack and set aside. Cook indomine according to package instruction until firm (you do not want the indomie to be overly soft)
  2. Add oil into a pan and heat over high .
  3. Add the shrimps and cook on all sides for about two minutes.
  4. Add in the vegetables and stir fry for a minute.
  5. Add in noodle seasoning and then noodles. Combine
